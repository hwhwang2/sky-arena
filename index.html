3 Players · One iPad

Sky Arena

Author: Max Hwang & Hoho Hwang

Three squadrons ring a circular arena, 120° apart — perfectly even ground no matter what shape the screen is. Tap a plane, pull back, release to fire. Ram enemies to damage them. Last squadron flying wins.

Red Falcons
3 HP · normal speed
Explosive — blast hits nearby enemies (and teammates if FF is on), never itself
Blue Needles
2 HP · fast, small
Rampage — can hit several planes per shot
Green Bulldogs
4 HP · slow, big
Heavy tank — leaves a poop trail that costs opponents 0.5 HP (never teammates)

Pass to Red Falcons

Hand the iPad over, then tap ready.
